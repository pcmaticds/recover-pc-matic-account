* Forgotten password.

Recovering your account helps restore access to your subscription and account management features.

====================================

Before attempting to recover your account, make sure you have:

* Access to your registered email account.
* A stable internet connection.
* Your account username or email address, if available.
* A compatible web browser.

Having this information ready can make the recovery process faster.

How to Recover Your PC Matic Account
====================================

Follow these general steps to recover your account.

Step One
--------

Open your preferred web browser and go to the PC Matic sign-in page.

Step Two
--------

Select the **Forgot Password** or **Account Recovery** option.

Step Three
----------

Enter the email address or username associated with your account.

Step Four
---------

Complete any required identity verification steps.

Step Five
---------

Check your registered email for account recovery instructions.

Step Six
--------

Follow the instructions to reset your password or restore account access.

Step Seven
----------

Sign in with your updated credentials after the recovery process is complete.

If you do not receive a recovery email, check your spam or junk folder before requesting another recovery message.

How to Reset Your Password
==========================

If you remember your account email but not your password, resetting your password is usually the quickest recovery option.

To create a secure password:

* Use uppercase and lowercase letters.
* Include numbers and special characters.
* Avoid common words or personal information.
* Do not reuse passwords from other accounts.

A strong password improves the security of your account.

Recovering Your Account Email
=============================

If you cannot remember the email associated with your account:

* Check previous account-related emails.
* Review saved passwords in your browser or password manager.
* Search your email inbox for earlier subscription or account messages.
* Verify that you are using the correct email address before starting the recovery process.

Finding the correct email address helps ensure a successful account recovery.

Common PC Matic Account Recovery Problems
=========================================

Recovery Email Not Received
---------------------------

If you do not receive the recovery message:

* Check your spam or junk folder.
* Confirm that you entered the correct email address.
* Wait a few minutes and try again.

Invalid Login Credentials
-------------------------

If your new password does not work:

* Double-check your username or email.
* Make sure Caps Lock is turned off.
* Enter the password carefully.

Unable to Verify Your Identity
------------------------------

If identity verification fails:

* Review the information you entered.
* Use the details associated with your account.
* Try the recovery process again.

Account Still Inaccessible
--------------------------

If you still cannot access your account after completing the recovery steps:

* Verify your account information.
* Repeat the recovery process if necessary.
* Use official support resources if additional assistance is required.

Tips to Keep Your PC Matic Account Secure

After recovering your account, follow these best practices:

* Create a strong, unique password.
* Update your recovery email if needed.
* Keep your contact information current.
* Store your login credentials securely.

* Avoid sharing your account credentials with others.

Good security habits help reduce the risk of future login problems.
